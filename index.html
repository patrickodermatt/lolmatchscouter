Matchscouter for LoL

Realtime Game Search for League of Legends

View on Google Play Store

Description

Quickfind all information about Summoners you play with.
See the current rating for your teammates and enemies. Use your advantage to climb the ladders!
Get realtime stats on your enemies and find out where they are weak.

Features

You’ll be able to see the following information about each player in the match:

- Tier, Rank and League Points
- Ranked Wins/Losses
- Ranked Winrate
- Ranked Wins/Losses with the current champion
- Ranked Winrate with the current champion
- Champion KDA
- Count of games that the player has played with the current champion (Ranked)
- Summonerlevel (if summoner is not level 30)
- Bans

Server

Only works on the following servers:
- Europe West
- North America
- Europe Nordic & East
- Korea
- Latin America North
- Latin America South
- Brazil
- Oceania
- Turkey
- Russia
